Osaka
Ramintra

Osaka brings the quiet elegance of Japanese architecture to Bangkok's northern corridor. Every home is designed with a deep respect for natural light, negative space, and the harmony between indoor and outdoor living — principles rooted in the Japanese aesthetic philosophy of wabi-sabi.

Set in a lush, low-density community near Ramintra and Kubon, Osaka offers spacious detached homes surrounded by mature trees and serene water features, creating a true retreat from the city.

  • Japanese-inspired architecture with natural material finishes
  • Spacious open-plan living with garden view
  • Clubhouse with onsen-style pool and relaxation garden
  • Low-density community — peaceful and private
  • Easy access to Ramintra expressway and DMK Airport
  • Near major hospitals, schools, and shopping centres
