The Nice
Rangsit

The Nice redefines townhome living with Japanese-inspired minimalism and thoughtful spatial design. Located in the heart of Rangsit — Klong 3, this community offers modern families an exceptional quality of life at an accessible price point.

Featuring clean lines, natural materials, and space-efficient layouts, each home maximises comfort and functionality. The community includes a shared clubhouse, gardens, and 24-hour security — everything a growing family needs.

  • Japanese-modern facade with natural wood accents
  • Open-plan kitchen and living on ground floor
  • 3 bedrooms, 2–3 bathrooms, 2-car parking
  • Energy-efficient design with good cross-ventilation
  • Clubhouse, communal garden, children's play area
  • Close to Future Park Rangsit and Thammasat University
