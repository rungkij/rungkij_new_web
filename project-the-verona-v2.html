Italian Elegance

The Verona
Rama 9

The Verona captures the romantic architecture of Verona, Italy — grand arches, refined stonework, and an enduring sense of classical beauty. Situated at Rama 9 — Bangkok Sports Club, these detached homes offer prestige living in one of Bangkok's most sought-after corridors.

Meticulously designed with spacious interiors, high-quality imported fittings, and lush private gardens, The Verona is the ideal home for those who demand both elegance and practicality in the heart of the city.

  • Italian-classical architecture with arched windows and cornices
  • High ceilings and premium marble flooring
  • Imported European kitchen and bathroom fixtures
  • Private garden with landscaped grounds
  • Smart home automation pre-installed
  • Centrally located with excellent transport links
