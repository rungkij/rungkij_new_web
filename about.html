Heritage

Over 50 Years
of Trust

V.S.K. Estate Company Limited has been a leading real estate developer in Thailand for over five decades. Operating under the RK and Rungkij brands, the company is led by Managing Director Visith Kittiudom, a visionary committed to creating exceptional residences.

We are dedicated to elevating the quality of life for every resident through thoughtful community development that meets contemporary lifestyle needs — from wellness and healthcare to education and sustainability.

With decades of accumulated expertise, we have delivered more than 15 residential projects, earning the trust of over 3,000 families across Bangkok and its surrounding areas.

Philosophy

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading real estate developer that elevates quality of life, creating sustainable and desirable communities for every family.

Mission

To develop high-quality residences in prime locations, designed with precision and built to international standards, delivering happiness to every client.

Values

Integrity, transparency, attention to detail, unwavering quality, innovation, and social and environmental responsibility in everything we do.

Milestones

A Journey
of Achievement

From our founding to today, we have grown steadily through sound management principles and an unwavering commitment to crafting quality homes.

Foundation

Established the real estate development business with a vision to create quality homes for Thai families.

Expansion
