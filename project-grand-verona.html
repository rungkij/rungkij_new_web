Grand Verona
Ramkhamhaeng

Grand Verona is Rungkij's flagship development — a meticulously designed community offering both premium detached houses and luxury townhomes in the heart of Bangkok's eastern corridor. Set within lush landscaped grounds, each residence is crafted to meet international standards of quality and elegance.

The project features a grand European-inspired entrance, a resort-style clubhouse, swimming pool, fitness centre, and a network of parks and greenways — creating a serene sanctuary amidst the vibrant city.

  • Grand European-style main gate and entrance boulevard
  • Resort clubhouse with pool, gym & function rooms
  • 24-hour security with CCTV throughout
  • Smart home pre-wiring on selected units
  • Lush landscaped gardens and parks
  • Close to expressways and Suvarnabhumi Airport
