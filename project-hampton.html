The Hampton
Rama 9

The Hampton draws inspiration from London's most prestigious residential neighbourhoods — classic elegance, refined proportions, and a deep commitment to quality craftsmanship. Located at the prestigious Rama 9 — Bangkok Sports Club corridor, these homes offer a rare combination of urban accessibility and estate-like serenity.

Each home is designed with generous floor-to-ceiling heights, premium imported finishes, and expansive private gardens — a true statement of sophisticated living in central Bangkok.

  • British-classic architecture with imported facade materials
  • Generous 4–5 metre ceiling heights in living areas
  • Premium imported kitchen and bathroom fixtures
  • Private garden with optional swimming pool
  • Smart home system with app control
  • 5 minutes to Asok, Phrom Phong, and Thonglor
